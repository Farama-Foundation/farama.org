How to Submit Projects

If you'd like your reinforcement learning project to us to be maintained long term, please reach out to us at jkterry@farama.org. In general, we're only looking for projects that have already become widely used or clearly have a profound technical use in the field if they're not currently widely used. We work to bring all new and existing projects to our standards, and generally request that new projects meet the vast majority of these requirements before submission (though please reach out to us if you have any questions).
